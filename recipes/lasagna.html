Lasagna

Description

To make a lasagna, start by preparing a rich meat sauce. In a large skillet, sauté chopped onions and garlic in olive oil until fragrant, then add ground beef or Italian sausage and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian herbs like basil and oregano, letting the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes to deepen the flavor. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, salt, pepper, and a handful of grated Parmesan. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then begin layering in a baking dish: start with a thin layer of sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella, and more sauce. Repeat until the dish is full, finishing with a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 40 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbling. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing to allow the layers to set.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500 g ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 800 g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • salt & pepper
  • 450 g ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • 9–12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Heat oil; sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add meat and brown.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs; simmer 20–30 min.
  4. Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt & pepper.
  5. Boil noodles al dente.
  6. Layer: sauce → noodles → ricotta → mozzarella; repeat.
  7. Top with remaining cheese.
  8. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for ~40 min; rest 10–15 min before slicing.
