Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer cubes, onions, and capsicum grilled to smoky perfection. The paneer is coated in a spiced yogurt mixture flavored with chili powder, turmeric, cumin, and garam masala, then grilled or roasted until lightly charred. It's juicy, flavorful, and pairs perfectly with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Ingredients

  • 250 g paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 onion, cubed
  • 1/2 capsicum, cubed

Steps

  1. Whisk yogurt with lemon juice, spices, salt, and oil.
  2. Add paneer, onion, and capsicum; mix gently.
  3. Marinate 30â€“60 minutes (or longer for more flavor).
  4. Thread onto skewers alternately.
  5. Grill, bake, or pan-fry until golden and slightly charred.
  6. Brush with butter and serve hot with mint chutney.
