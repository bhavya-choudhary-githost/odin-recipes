Pizza

Description

A classic pizza features a crisp, chewy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and a touch of olive oil. The dough is kneaded until smooth and left to rise for a light texture. Once stretched, it’s layered with sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings, then baked at high heat until golden and bubbly — simple, timeless, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Optional: sliced onions, capsicum, olives, or pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil; knead into a smooth dough.
  3. Cover and let rise 1–2 hours until doubled.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out dough into a circle and place on a tray.
  6. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  7. Top with mozzarella and chosen toppings.
  8. Bake 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbly.
  9. Slice and serve hot.
